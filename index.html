About

Designing learning programs that grow careers and organisational capability

At Generation, a global employment training organisation, I've led the design, implementation, and evaluation of workforce development programs across eight different roles and industry levels. Most recently, I've specialised in climate careers, designing comprehensive 16-week programs for Retrofit Advisors and Heat Pump Installers that transformed people with no prior experience into qualified clean energy professionals. 85% of graduates landed jobs within three months.

I work across the full learning product lifecycle: scoping and sequencing, lesson design, assessment, learner feedback analysis, and iteration. I collaborate closely with subject-matter experts to establish measurable learning objectives, then build blended programs (self-paced modules, live virtual sessions, and in-person workshops) with the goal of preparing learners for industry qualifications and accelerating careers.

I'm also a director of London Green Wood, a community greenwood carving cooperative where I design curriculum for seven courses reaching 700+ participants annually. I'm a climber, an environmental education volunteer, and someone who believes that reconnecting people with the natural world is a foundation for leadership and action. I bring that same ethos to my work, designing programs that build technical skills and agency while inspiring a deeper sense of ownership over the problems that matter most.

Selected Work

Case studies

Green Tech · Generation UK

Retrofit Advisor Training Program

16-week blended program · Lead Curriculum Designer
+

Challenge

Millions of UK homes need energy-efficiency retrofitting, creating urgent demand for Retrofit Advisors, a role with no established training curriculum. The program needed to meet industry certification standards while preparing diverse learners for immediate employment.

Approach

  • AI-driven needs analysis aligned with real-time green tech industry requirements
  • Interdisciplinary curriculum: technical sessions, soft skills, and community-based learning
  • Roleplay and case-study lessons to build practical advisory skills
  • Assessments benchmarked against industry certification standards
  • Close collaboration with SMEs and technical training specialists
Outcome

85%+ of graduates employed with green jobs employers within 3 months. Contributed to 270+ hours of blended learning content including live instruction, online modules, and practical workshops.

K–12 / EdTech · Distinctive Schools (IL & MI)

Distance Learning Transformation

2-year engagement · Digital Curriculum & Instructional Design Specialist
+

Challenge

Serving high-poverty communities across Illinois and Michigan, Distinctive Schools needed to pivot to remote learning while maintaining engagement for 1,000+ students, compounded by limited tech access and varied digital literacy among families and staff.

Approach

  • Developed and deployed remote curriculum solutions network-wide
  • Designed professional development for 150 teachers on remote instruction
  • Led an Innovation Challenge with 500+ teachers, parents, and students
  • Personalised learning paths using student data; coached 15 teachers weekly
Outcome

Schools ranked 74th percentile for engagement and 89th percentile for culture among high-poverty US schools. In a prior teaching role at the same network, improved student achievement from 37% → 64% in math and 19% → 71% in reading.

Green Tech · Generation UK

Heat Pump Installer Training Program

Ongoing development · Curriculum Designer
+

Challenge

The UK's push toward heat pump adoption requires a large workforce of qualified installers, but industry standards are evolving rapidly. The curriculum needed to keep pace with changing regulations while delivering practical, hands-on competency.

Approach

  • Managed project scoping and sequencing aligned with evolving industry standards
  • Developed course outlines and learning outcomes with industry SMEs
  • Integrated practical workshop components with online module delivery
  • Built flexible frameworks for rapid content updates as standards shifted
Outcome

Delivered a modular, future-proof curriculum framework that adapts to evolving industry certification requirements while maintaining consistency across cohorts.
